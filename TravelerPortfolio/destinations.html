My Destinations

Explore the places that have captured my heart and shaped my journey

Iceland

Northern Europe

A land of fire and ice where waterfalls cascade down volcanic cliffs and geothermal hot springs steam against snowy backdrops.

Travel Tip: Rent a 4x4 vehicle to explore the highlands and bring waterproof clothing - the weather changes rapidly!

Bali

Indonesia

An island paradise where lush jungles meet pristine beaches, and ancient temples stand alongside modern wellness retreats.

Travel Tip: Visit Ubud during the early morning to experience the peaceful rice terraces without crowds.

Japan

East Asia

A fascinating blend of ancient traditions and cutting-edge technology, where meticulously manicured gardens neighbor neon-lit skyscrapers.

Travel Tip: Purchase a Japan Rail Pass before arriving to save money on bullet train travel between cities.

Italy

Southern Europe

A country where art, history, and cuisine create an intoxicating blend of experiences from the canals of Venice to the ruins of Rome.

Travel Tip: Skip the tourist restaurants and look for places where locals eat - follow the rule: no pictures on the menu!

Morocco

North Africa

A sensory feast where vibrant souks, ancient medinas, and sweeping desert landscapes create an unforgettable cultural tapestry.

Travel Tip: Hire a local guide when exploring the medinas - they're maze-like and easy to get lost in!

New Zealand

Oceania

A natural wonderland where dramatic mountains, pristine lakes, and lush forests create the perfect backdrop for adventure.

Travel Tip: Allow more time than you think for road trips - you'll want to stop frequently for the incredible views!
