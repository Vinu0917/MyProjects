Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Travelexia's Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, process, and disclose your information, including personal information, in conjunction with your access to and use of our travel services.

When you use our services, you trust us with your information. We are committed to keeping that trust. That starts with helping you understand our privacy practices.

2. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, postal address, payment information, and government identification.
  • Travel Information: Travel itineraries, booking details, passport information, and travel preferences.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, including browsing history, clicks, and time spent on pages.
  • Device Information: Information about the device you use to access our services, including hardware model, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Processing and managing your travel bookings
  • Providing customer support
  • Improving and personalizing our services
  • Sending promotional communications and updates
  • Ensuring the security of our services
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Travel Suppliers: Airlines, hotels, car rental companies, and other service providers necessary to complete your booking.
  • Payment Processors: To process your payments securely.
  • Business Partners: Companies we partner with to offer integrated services.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access your personal information
  • The right to correct inaccurate information
  • The right to delete your information
  • The right to restrict or object to processing
  • The right to data portability

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, and processed in, countries other than the country in which you are resident. These countries may have data protection laws that are different from the laws of your country.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
